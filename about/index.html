About

Digital Media Arts • Illustration • Concept • Animation

I love bringing original concepts to life in Illustration and Animation.

Hello!

My name is John and I’m a 2D illustrator specializing in character illustration for games, animation, VTubers, and more. I love consuming anime, art, and media from all kinds of sources, whether it’s learning from a fellow artist or drawing inspiration from my favorite shows, music, and visual storytelling. Currently, I’m a Digital Media Arts major at San Jose State University, continuing to grow my skills and creative voice.

Here you’ll find class projects, personal experiments, and interactive pieces as I explore storytelling across games, animation, and the web.

For collaborations or questions, feel free to reach out via email or any of the social links below.
